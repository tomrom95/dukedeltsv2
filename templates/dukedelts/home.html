ABOUT US


Welcome

Welcome to the Delta Kappa chapter of Delta Tau Delta Fraternity at Duke University. Our chapter was founded in 1922 and rechartered in 2003, making us one of the oldest chapters at Duke University. As Delts, we are committed to leading lives of excellence in all aspects of life. We set the highest standards for leadership, academics, athletics, and campus and community involvement.

If fraternity life is something that you may like to get involved in during your time at Duke, please visit our section for potential members. We are constantly looking for outstanding gentlemen who exemplify our core values and standards.




History

Delta Tau Delta is a national fraternity predicated on the values of truth, courage, faith and power. We consider five main principles as our most important obligations: 1) Academic Achievement 2) Active Involvement 3) Honorable Conduct 4) Financial Integrity, and 5) Recruitment. We, in 1886, merged with the Rainbow Fraternity, an ancient and respectable southern fraternity whose values coincided with our own. Our fraternity is divided into five regions across the United States of America: Northern, Eastern, Southern, Western Pacific, and the Western Plains divisions. Delta Tau Deltaâ€™s Alpha Chapters are located at Bethany College, Jefferson College, Allegheny College, and Ohio Wesleyan University. Our national community service is the Juvenile Diabetes Research Foundation (JDRF) and our Central Office is located in Fishers, Indiana after initially being located in New York City, New York.

The Delta Kappa chapter of Delta Tau Delta at Duke University was founded in 1928 making it one of the first fraternities to exist at Duke. After a hiatus, the Delta Kappa chapter was re-founded in 2003. Since our presence began at Duke University in the early twentieth century, we have initiated 1,418 men, a clear indication of our strong presence and everlasting legacy of our longstanding brotherhood.

RECRUITMENT

The brothers of Delta Tau Delta are committed to recruitment 365 days a year. We are always looking for young men that embody our motto: "committed to lives of excellence". Our brothers are leaders in all aspects of campus life, from academics to athletics to community service and philanthropy. Our new members are responsible for maintaining this standard of excellence and upholding the traditions of the Fraternity.

At Duke, the recruitment is deferred to the beginning of the Spring semester to allow ample time to get acclimated to the university setting. Once the recruitment process formally begins, potential new members will be given an opportunity to socialize with current brothers and see all that our chapter (Delta Kappa) has to offer. Our section is located in Decker Tower (also known as Edens 3A) on the first, third and fourth floors.

If you are interested in joining the brotherhood of Delta Kappa, please fill out the following form to be placed on the Rush Committee's contact list. To receive more information on Delta Tau Delta, Delta Kappa please contact any of our recruitment chairs.
